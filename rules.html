PrismPeak Server Rules

  • Build Houses: You can build your house 700 TO 1500 Blocks away from the spawn area.
  • No Killing: PrismPeak is a peaceful server. PvP and killing other players are strictly prohibited.
  • Respect Others: Treat all players with respect and kindness. Bullying, harassment, or discrimination will not be tolerated.
  • No Griefing: Destroying or altering another player's builds without permission is forbidden.
  • No Stealing: Taking items from other players without their consent is not allowed.
  • Keep Chat Clean: Avoid using inappropriate language, spamming, or advertising other servers in the chat.
  • Follow Staff Instructions: Listen to and follow the directions given by server staff members.
  • No Hacking or Cheating: Using mods, hacks, or exploits to gain an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.
  • Build Responsibly: Ensure your builds do not negatively impact the environment or other players' experiences.
  • Report Issues: If you encounter any problems or witness rule-breaking, report it to a staff member immediately.
  • Have Fun: Enjoy your time on PrismPeak and contribute to a positive and friendly community!
